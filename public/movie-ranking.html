The Best Movies According to Wolfgang

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Forrest Gump

This is my favorite movie. I love how it has everything; a good story, comedy, love, and life lessons.

"Life is like a box of chocolates. You never know what you're gonna get."

Spirited Away

A visually stunning film with some of the most imaginative characters I've ever seen.

The Princess Bride

Bosting unforgettable characters and a timeless adventure, what's not to love about this classic of a film.
